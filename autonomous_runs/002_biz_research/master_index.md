## Tier 1: Developed Ideas (have detailed plans, pricing, or research)

### 1. Digital Asset Landlord (Etsy/Creative Fabrica)
- **Source**: Docs 1, 2, 4
- **Stage**: Deep research complete, no implementation
- **Target Market**: B2C — crafters, teachers, designers, hobbyists buying digital downloads
- **Revenue Model**: Volume-based digital asset sales on marketplaces (Etsy listing fees + commissions, Creative Fabrica subscription pool, Freepik PPD, Adobe Stock licensing)
- **Key Tech**: n8n automation, AI image generation (fal.ai, Flux), metadata generation, batch upload pipelines
- **Estimated Costs**: $0.20/listing on Etsy + API generation costs (~$0.01-0.10/asset). Creative Fabrica is free to list.
- **Key Insight**: "Statistical Floor" model — 10,000 assets at $0.50/month avg = $5K/month. 70% do nothing, 10% are winners.
- **Development Level**: Detailed marketplace analysis, tool comparison (EverBee, eRank, SaleSamurai, ProfitTree), production pipeline architecture, unit economics modeled

### 2. Optic Stage (AI Visual Production Agency)
- **Source**: Doc 5
- **Stage**: Website live, Stripe set up, email drafts written, lead sourcing methods documented. Pre-revenue.
- **Target Market**: B2B — restaurants (food photos), real estate agents (virtual staging), car dealers (showroom backgrounds)
- **Revenue Model**: Service-based — per-asset pricing ($30-90/asset depending on niche and volume)
- **Key Tech**: AI image upscaling (fal.ai, WaveSpeed), n8n for outreach automation, Instantly.ai for cold email at scale
- **Estimated Costs**: ~$50-100/month (domains, email tools, API credits)
- **Key Insight**: Manual outreach first (5 personalized samples per niche), automate after first 3-5 clients
- **Development Level**: Full business plan, pricing tiers, outreach templates, lead sourcing playbook, domain + email set up

### 3. Infinite AI Content Studio (Self-Hosted)
- **Source**: Doc 6
- **Stage**: Detailed blueprint, no implementation
- **Target Market**: Internal use (powers other businesses) — could also sell as a service
- **Revenue Model**: Cost savings (eliminates API taxes) — enables other revenue models
- **Key Tech**: Hostinger VPS (n8n brain) + RunPod GPU (ComfyUI muscle), Network Volumes for persistence, LTX-2/Wan 2.1/Flux/InfinityTalk models
- **Estimated Costs**: ~$20/month ($8 VPS + $7 storage + $5 GPU time)
- **Key Insight**: "Intermittent" pattern — GPU only runs during generation. Sub-workflow architecture ("cheap APIs") is the right starting approach
- **Development Level**: Full infrastructure blueprint, cost analysis, sub-workflow roadmap, specific model recommendations

### 4. MagAI Cold Email Campaign
- **Source**: Doc 3
- **Stage**: Detailed plan with email drafts written, audience segments defined, Apollo.io links ready
- **Target Market**: Business owners using AI, marketing agencies, college students
- **Revenue Model**: Affiliate — 20% lifetime recurring commission on MagAI subscriptions ($20-40+/month)
- **Key Tech**: Instantly.ai for sending, Apollo.io for leads, n8n for automation, EmailListVerify
- **Estimated Costs**: ~$300 first month ($195 domains + $97 Instantly + $9 leads), ~$256/month ongoing
- **Key Insight**: 300 emails/day across 3 domains, 15 mailboxes. 1-2 emails per lead max. Very detailed ICP targeting.
- **Development Level**: Full campaign plan, 3 ICPs with email copy (2 variants each), cost breakdown, infrastructure spec

### 5. Recurring SaaS Affiliate Pipeline
- **Source**: Doc 3
- **Stage**: Business plan written, tool stack defined
- **Target Market**: SaaS users, small businesses, creators
- **Revenue Model**: Recurring affiliate commissions (GoHighLevel, Systeme.io, Fliki, BasedLabs, MagAI)
- **Key Tech**: n8n automation, cold email (Smartlead/Instantly), SEO content on Medium/Reddit/Quora
- **Estimated Costs**: ~$200-300/month (email tools, domains, lead databases)
- **Key Insight**: Dual-channel approach — cold email for quick wins, SEO content for compounding organic traffic
- **Development Level**: Executive summary, tool stack, marketing strategy outline, platform-specific tactics
