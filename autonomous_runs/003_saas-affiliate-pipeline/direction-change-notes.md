## Why This Pivot Makes Sense

### Feasibility
- Zero capital requirement (all free platforms + tools)
- Existing VPS handles all automation (n8n, Shlink, content)
- Claude provides content creation capability at $0
- 24 vetted programs with instant/easy approval available

### Revenue Model
- Recurring commissions compound: each referral pays monthly for years
- Average SaaS customer lifetime: 24-36 months
- Single $100/mo tool referral at 30% = $30/mo for 2-3 years = $720-$1,080 total
- 35 active referrals = $1,000/mo recurring

### Skills Match
- Content creation (Claude-assisted) = primary skill being developed
- n8n automation already available on VPS
- No design, video production, or client management needed initially
- Learning curve is content marketing, not technical development

### Market Timing
- SaaS affiliate market growing at 15.6% CAGR
- 70% of SaaS providers now offer lifetime affiliate payouts
- AI content tools make production 40% faster
- Free platforms (LinkedIn, Medium, YouTube) have better organic reach than ever
