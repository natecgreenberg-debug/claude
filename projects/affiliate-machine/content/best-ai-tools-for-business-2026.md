## AI for Content and Video Creation

### Fliki — Best for AI Video at Scale

If you're producing video content and you're not using an AI video tool, you're either rich enough to hire a production team or you're leaving a lot of content on the table.

[Fliki](https://fliki.ai/?via=nathaniel-greenberg) is the best balance of output quality and ease of use I've found for AI video creation. The core workflow: write (or paste) a script, choose a voice from their library of 1,000+ realistic AI voices, and Fliki assembles a video with relevant B-roll footage automatically.

What it does well:
- **Realistic voice cloning** — the AI voices sound genuinely human, not robotic
- **Automatic B-roll selection** — it pulls relevant stock footage based on your script content
- **Talking avatar videos** — choose from AI avatars or upload your own face
- **Multi-language support** — 75+ languages for international content
- **Script-to-video in minutes** — a 3-minute video takes 10-15 minutes to produce

Pricing: $28/month (Basic), $88/month (Standard — recommended for serious content creators). The Standard plan gives you longer videos, more renders per month, and 4K quality output.

Use case sweet spot: YouTube explainer videos, social media content, product demonstrations, online course content, and any situation where you need to produce video at volume without being on camera yourself.

[Try Fliki here](https://fliki.ai/?via=nathaniel-greenberg) — the free plan lets you export short videos with watermarks to get a feel for the output quality before committing.

**Pros:**
- Output quality is genuinely good (not obviously AI on most content)
- Massive voice library
- Time savings are real — 10x faster than traditional video production
- No video editing skills required

**Cons:**
- Stock footage selections can be generic or mismatched — always review before publishing
- Complex talking head videos still look AI-generated to careful observers
- The $88/month plan is necessary for serious use

### BasedLabs — Best for AI Image and Video Generation

[BasedLabs](https://www.basedlabs.ai/?via=nathaniel) is a generative AI platform built for creators who need custom images and video clips — not stock footage, but genuinely original generated content.

What makes BasedLabs stand out is the breadth of models available. You're not locked into one generation style. The platform gives you access to multiple underlying models (Flux, SDXL variants, and others) with a single subscription, which means you can generate photorealistic product images, stylized illustrations, and video clips all in one place.

For businesses, the main use cases:
- **Product mockup images** — generate lifestyle shots of products without a photo shoot
- **Social media creative** — custom illustrations and graphics at scale
- **Ad creative variations** — generate 20 variations of an image concept in minutes
- **Short video clips** — generate B-roll or motion graphics from text prompts
- **Brand asset creation** — logos, backgrounds, brand imagery

Pricing: $12/month (Starter), $29/month (Pro), $60/month (Ultra). The 30% recurring commission affiliate program is one of the better ones in the AI tools space.

At $12/month for the Starter plan, [BasedLabs](https://www.basedlabs.ai/?via=nathaniel) is one of the most affordable ways to access quality AI image generation. Comparable tools like Midjourney run $10-30/month with less model variety.

**Pros:**
- Access to multiple generation models
- Competitive pricing
- Good for both image and video generation
- Simple interface, fast outputs

**Cons:**

- Video generation is still catching up to dedicated tools
- Inpainting/editing tools are more limited than some competitors

## AI for Writing and Content

### Claude / ChatGPT — The Foundation

Any AI tool roundup that doesn't mention the large language models directly is avoiding the obvious. Claude (Anthropic) and ChatGPT (OpenAI) are the foundational AI writing tools, and for most text-based work, they're the right starting point before you reach for specialized tools.

For business use cases:
- First drafts of emails, proposals, and copy
- Summarizing long documents, reports, or meetings
- Generating options for headlines, CTAs, and ad angles
- Research synthesis and competitive analysis

At $20/month for either, these are the highest-leverage AI tools for knowledge workers. If you're not using one regularly, start there before anything else on this list.

## AI for Audio and Transcription

### Whisper / Descript — Best for Audio Workflows

If you produce podcasts, record meetings, or create audio content, Whisper (free, via OpenAI) and Descript ($24/month) are both worth knowing.

Whisper transcribes audio with near-human accuracy across multiple languages and is free to use via API or several frontends. Descript wraps transcription into an editing environment where you edit video by editing the transcript — genuinely novel and fast.
