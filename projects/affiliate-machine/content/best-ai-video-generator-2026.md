# Best AI Video Generator in 2026: The Tools That Actually Deliver

AI video generation has gone from gimmick to genuinely useful in the past two years. ## The Landscape in 2026

AI video tools fall into a few distinct categories:

**Text-to-video:** Type a script, get a video with voiceover, visuals, and music. Best for content creators who need volume.

**Avatar-based video:** AI presenters deliver your script. Best for product demos, training, and corporate communications.

**Image/clip enhancement:** Transform still images or raw footage with AI effects, transitions, and stylization.

**Generative video:** Completely AI-generated video from a text or image prompt — the most experimental category.

The best tools increasingly blur these lines. Here's what's worth your attention.
