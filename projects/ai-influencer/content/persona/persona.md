### 2. Brain Fog & Focus
The #1 fear, especially around work performance. Deeply underreported outside Reddit.

- Creatine for cognitive function — the emerging research

- Sleep tracking: what to actually look for in menopause
- When HRT fixes sleep vs. when it doesn't

- Cortisol and stress weight — the cortisol-belly connection

- Creatine for muscle preservation — the underreported benefit
