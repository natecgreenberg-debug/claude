# Revenue Math: Path to $10K/Month with AI Influencers

**Date:** 2026-03-12
**Scope:** Worst-case and realistic-case math for 5 AI influencer accounts posting short-form video, monetizing via info products (~$25 price point)

---

## Key Assumptions & Definitions

- **Business model:** Short-form video (TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts) → link in bio → landing page → $25 info product
- **Scale:** 5 AI influencer accounts × 3–5 videos/day × 3 platforms
- **Goal:** $10,000/month net revenue
- **Monetization only:** Info product sales (not ad revenue — ad RPM on Shorts is $0.02–$0.30/1K views, far too low to be the primary vehicle)

---

## Platform View Benchmarks for New Accounts

### TikTok
- **Worst case (floor):** 200–500 views per video for new accounts with zero following
- **Median new account:** 2,000–5,000 views per video (algorithm-assisted distribution)
- **Breakout potential:** Any video can hit 10K–100K+ regardless of follower count; TikTok boosts new accounts ~12% in first week
- Source: socialinsider.io TikTok Benchmarks 2025, admanage.ai

### Instagram Reels
- **Worst case:** 100–300 views per Reel on a new account
- **Median new account:** 500–2,000 views
- **Engagement rate:** 0.65% (lowest of the three platforms)
- Source: loopexdigital.com Instagram Reels Statistics 2026

### YouTube Shorts
- **Worst case:** 50–200 views for a new channel
- **Median new account:** 500–2,000 views (platform boosts new content)
- **Engagement rate:** ~5.9% (highest retention and engagement of the three)
- **Notable:** 50-60 second Shorts average 4.1M views *across all accounts*; this is skewed by established channels
- Source: zebracat.ai YouTube Shorts Statistics 2025, autofaceless.ai

## The Conversion Funnel (Worst Case)

| Stage | Worst-Case Rate | Notes |
|-------|----------------|-------|
| Video views → Profile visits | 1% | Low for new accounts; industry range is 1–5% |
| Profile visits → Bio link clicks | 10% | Industry average bio link CTR is 2–3% of views; modeled as 10% of profile visitors |
| Effective views → Bio link click | ~0.5–1% | Combined: 1% × 50% (not all profile visitors click link) |
| Bio link → Landing page load | 80% | Accounting for drop-off between click and page load |
| Landing page → Purchase ($25) | 0.5% | Conservative; industry average for cold traffic is 1–3% |

**Effective worst-case conversion: Views → Sale = 0.004% (4 per 100,000 views)**

Breaking this down in full chain:
- 100,000 views
- → 1,000 profile visits (1%)
- → 100 bio link clicks (10% of profile visitors)
- → 80 landing page arrivals
- → **0.4 sales** (0.5% conversion)

Rounding up: **~1 sale per 250,000 views** at worst case.
At $25/sale: **$25 per 250,000 views** = **$0.10 per 1,000 views (RPV)** — pure info product revenue, worst case.

## The Conversion Funnel (Realistic/Median Case)

| Stage | Realistic Rate | Notes |
|-------|---------------|-------|
| Video views → Profile visits | 3% | Mid-range for engaged short-form content |
| Profile visits → Bio link clicks | 25% | Warmed audience; compelling CTA |
| Bio link → Landing page load | 90% | |
| Landing page → Purchase ($25) | 1.5% | Industry median for warm traffic |

**Realistic conversion: ~1 sale per 9,000 views**
At $25/sale: **$2.78 per 1,000 views (RPV)**

## Volume Math: How Many Views to Hit $10K/Month?

### Worst Case

```
Target: $10,000/month
Revenue per sale: $25
Sales needed: 400/month

Views per sale (worst case): 250,000
Total views needed: 400 × 250,000 = 100,000,000 views/month
```

**100 million views/month needed at worst case.**

### Realistic Case

```
Views per sale (realistic): 9,000
Total views needed: 400 × 9,000 = 3,600,000 views/month
```

**3.6 million views/month at median benchmarks.**

---

## Volume Achievable: 5 Accounts × 3 Platforms × Daily Posting

### Worst-Case View Scenario (new accounts, all underperform)

```
5 accounts × 3 platforms = 15 posting channels
15 channels × 4 videos/day = 60 videos/day
60 videos/day × 30 days = 1,800 videos/month

Average views (worst case): 300 views/video
Total views: 1,800 × 300 = 540,000 views/month
```

At worst-case RPV ($0.10/1K views):
**$54/month** — far below $10K.

This is the honest answer: at true worst case, this does not work in month 1.

### Realistic Case (accounts gain some traction by month 3–6)

```
60 videos/day × 30 days = 1,800 videos/month
Average views (post-warmup): 2,000 views/video
Total views: 1,800 × 2,000 = 3,600,000 views/month
```

At realistic RPV ($2.78/1K views):
**$10,008/month** — hits the target.

**The realistic case is achievable, but requires:**
1. Accounts to warm up to ~2,000 average views/video (typically takes 60–90 days)
2. At least one or two videos per account per week catching algorithmic distribution (5K–50K views)
3. Consistent posting at the stated volume (4 videos/day × 5 accounts × 3 platforms)

---

## Breakout Scenario (1 or 2 Viral Videos/Week)

Short-form video has high variance. A single video hitting 100K–500K views radically changes the math.

```
Base volume: 3,600,000 views/month (from above)
+ 4 breakout videos/month at 200,000 views each = 800,000 bonus views
Total: 4,400,000 views/month

At realistic RPV: 4,400,000/1,000 × $2.78 = $12,232/month
```

Realistic with occasional viral hits: **$12K–$20K/month**.

## Critical Assumptions & Caveats

1. **Product-market fit is assumed.** Bad offer → conversion rate collapses to <0.1%. Getting the info product and landing page right is the highest-leverage variable in this entire funnel.
2. **Platform distribution is not guaranteed.** New accounts can be stuck in "sandbox" mode for 2–4 weeks on TikTok before the algorithm tests their content.
3. **The $25 price point is the floor.** Moving to $37 or $47 increases revenue 48–88% with no volume change. This is the single easiest lever.
4. **Email capture multiplies LTV.** If even 5% of landing page visitors capture an email but don't buy, an email sequence can convert 10–20% of those leads over 30 days, adding 0.5% to overall conversion.
5. **Compounding effect.** As follower counts grow, RPV improves because warm followers have higher CTR and conversion rates than cold algorithm-served traffic.

---

## Summary: The Math in Plain Language

To hit $10K/month selling a $25 info product at median benchmarks:
- Need ~400 sales/month
- Need ~3.6M views/month across all accounts/platforms
- Achievable with 5 accounts × 3 platforms × 4 videos/day posting 2,000 avg views/video
- **Timeline: 5–6 months to reach this scale from scratch**
- Bumping price to $47: same traffic target delivers **$18,800/month**

The volume math works. The bottleneck is (1) getting accounts past the cold start phase, and (2) having a product that actually converts at 1.5%+.
