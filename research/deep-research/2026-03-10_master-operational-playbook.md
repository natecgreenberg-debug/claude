## Strategy Overview

### The Core Thesis
SaaS affiliate programs offer 20-70% recurring commissions. A single referral to a $100/mo tool at 30% commission = $30/mo for the lifetime of the customer. Average customer lifetime in SaaS is 24-36 months. That single referral = $720-$1,080 total. Get 35 active referrals across multiple programs, and you have $1,000/mo recurring.

### Why This Works with $0 Investment
- All platforms are free to join
- All affiliate programs are free to join
- Content creation tools (Canva, OBS, DaVinci Resolve) are free
- n8n automation is free (self-hosted on existing VPS)
- AI content assistance available via Claude free tier
- The only investment is time: 1-2 hours/day

### Market Validation
- SaaS affiliate market CAGR: 15.6% (2024-2028)
- 54% of SaaS marketers rank affiliate in top 3 acquisition strategies
- Referral leads convert at 3.63% (vs 0.78% traditional)
- 69% of affiliate marketers use SEO as primary traffic source
- Average affiliate earns $8,038/year; top 10% earn $73,000+/year

## First Sale Action Plan

**Goal**: Earn first recurring commission within 60 days.
**Focus**: ONE program (Systeme.io) + TWO platforms (LinkedIn + Medium)

### Week 1: Foundation
- [ ] Apply to Tier 1 programs (all 8 — takes 1 hour total)
- [ ] Set up LinkedIn profile as "marketing strategist" or "business tools expert"
- [ ] Create Medium account
- [ ] Create landing page/blog (WordPress, Ghost, or Carrd) with affiliate links
- [ ] Set up UTM parameter naming convention in Google Sheets
- [ ] Install Shlink on VPS for link tracking

### Week 2: First Content
- [ ] Write comparison article: "Systeme.io vs ClickFunnels" (Medium)
- [ ] Write review: "My Honest Systeme.io Review 2026" (Medium)
- [ ] Post 3 LinkedIn posts about funnel building / email marketing
- [ ] Begin Reddit karma building (5 comments/day, no links)
- [ ] Answer 3 Quora questions about funnel builders / email tools

### Week 3-4: Distribution
- [ ] Submit Medium articles to relevant publications
- [ ] Share blog posts on LinkedIn (link in first comment, not caption)
- [ ] Continue Reddit engagement (building toward 500+ karma)
- [ ] Write tutorial: "How to Build a Sales Funnel for Free" (Medium/blog)
- [ ] Answer 5 more Quora questions with blog post links

### Week 5-8: Scale Content
- [ ] Publish 2-3 articles/week on Medium
- [ ] Maintain 3-5 LinkedIn posts/week
- [ ] Start sharing relevant content on Reddit (once karma > 500)
- [ ] Track all link performance via UTM + Google Analytics
- [ ] Focus content on buyer-intent keywords: "best [tool] for [use case]", "[tool] review 2026", "[tool A] vs [tool B]"

### First Sale Accelerators
- **Systeme.io has a free plan** — recommend it genuinely as "best free funnel builder"
- **Free-to-paid conversion** is 2-3x more effective than direct sales pitch
- **Personal experience stories** convert better than feature lists
- **Comparison content** meets buyers at decision point (highest conversion format)

- The pipeline accelerates over time, not linearly

## Content Production System

### Daily Workflow (1-2 hours)
| Time | Activity | Output |
|------|----------|--------|
| 30 min | Draft article using Claude | 1 article draft |
| 30 min | Edit, add voice, fact-check | 1 publishable article |
| 15 min | LinkedIn/Quora posts | 1-2 social posts |
| 15 min | Reddit engagement | 3-5 helpful comments |

### Weekly Targets
- 2-3 long-form articles (Medium + blog)
- 5 LinkedIn posts (mix of carousels + text)
- 5 Quora answers
- 15+ Reddit comments (genuine engagement)
- 1-2 YouTube videos (when channel active)

### Content Template Priority
1. **Comparison articles** ("X vs Y") — highest conversion
2. **Honest reviews** — builds trust, converts decision-stage buyers
3. **"Best X for Y" lists** — captures broad traffic
4. **Tutorials** — provides value, natural tool recommendations
5. **Video reviews** — for YouTube, repurpose to shorts

### AI Content Rules
- Use AI for research, outlines, first drafts
- Always add personal voice, unique insights
- Fact-check every claim
- Publish gradually (2-3/week, never bulk dumps)
- Disclose AI assistance where required (Medium, YouTube)
- Google does NOT penalize quality AI-assisted content
- Medium BANS AI content created for SEO/affiliate purposes

## Risk Management

### Platform Risk
- Never put all revenue on one platform
- Maintain 3+ active platforms at all times
- Accept that account bans are an operational risk
- Keep content backed up locally

### Content Risk
- Follow each platform's specific AI content policy
- Always disclose affiliate relationships (FTC requirement)
- Use rel="sponsored" on affiliate links
- Avoid sudden publishing spikes (triggers spam filters)

### Revenue Risk
- Diversify across 5+ affiliate programs
- Mix recurring + one-time commission programs
- Monitor programs for payment reliability issues
- Avoid Awin/ShareASale for primary programs (payment complaints in 2026)

### Legal Risk
- Multi-account operations violate TOS (not criminal law)
- Consequence = account ban, not prosecution
- FTC disclosure is mandatory for all affiliate content
- EU AI Act (August 2026) may require AI content labeling
