[Verified: Multiple review sites + Trustpilot, 2026]

### Caution (Network-Based)
- **Awin/ShareASale**: Multiple Trustpilot complaints in early 2026 — $3,000 "approved and uncleared" balances, payment delays, bureaucratic excuses. Physical checks slow to process.
[Community: Trustpilot reviews, 2026]

### Best Practices
- Prefer programs with direct payment (not through networks)
- Watch for minimum payout thresholds ($50-200 range common)
- Net-30 to Net-60 payment terms are standard
- PayPal is fastest; wire transfers have higher minimums
