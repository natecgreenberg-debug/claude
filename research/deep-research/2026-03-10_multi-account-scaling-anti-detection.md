## Account Creation at Scale

### Gmail/Google Account Limits
- **4 accounts per phone number** maximum
- **5-10 accounts per day per IP** before temporary block
- Google monitors: IP addresses, device fingerprints, behavioral patterns, creation rate
- Emulator-based methods from 2024 no longer work in 2026
[Verified: multilogin.com + dicloak.com + pixelscan.net, 2026]

### Requirements for Scale
1. **Residential proxies**: Different IP per account ($5-15/GB from providers like Bright Data, Smartproxy)
2. **Anti-detect browser**: Separate browser profile per account
3. **Phone verification**: Services like SMSPool, 5SIM for disposable numbers ($0.10-0.50 per verification)
4. **Account warming**: New accounts need 1-2 weeks of organic activity before any promotional use
5. **Recovery emails**: Use unique recovery emails per account

### Social Media Account Limits
- **Reddit**: No phone requirement for creation, but karma building takes 2-4 weeks per account
- **Medium**: Email-only signup, but quality metrics gate distribution
- **LinkedIn**: Phone verification often required, profile completeness matters
- **TikTok**: Phone or email signup, 1,000 followers needed for bio link per account
- **YouTube**: Google account required, YPP per-channel requirements

## How Platforms Detect Multiple Accounts

### Detection Layers (2026)
1. **Browser fingerprinting** (50+ parameters): Canvas, WebGL, audio, fonts, screen resolution, timezone, language, plugins, device info
2. **IP analysis**: IP type classification (residential/mobile/datacenter), geolocation matching, DNS leak detection, WebRTC leak detection
3. **Behavioral analysis**: Login patterns, posting rhythms, interaction patterns, typing speed
4. **Session consistency**: Browser fingerprint + IP + behavior must be consistent across sessions
5. **Cookie/storage tracking**: Cross-site tracking via persistent identifiers
6. **Device fingerprinting**: Hardware-level identifiers beyond browser
[Verified: coronium.io + todetect.net + fingerprint.com, 2026]

### What Triggers Detection
- Same Canvas/WebGL rendering across accounts
- IP address shared between accounts
- Similar posting schedules or content patterns
- Inconsistent fingerprint-to-IP matching (e.g., US fingerprint + EU proxy)
- Sudden bulk account creation
- Cookie sharing between profiles
[Blog: browserless.io, 2026]

### Evolution in 2026
- Platforms no longer use single-point detection — they assess fingerprint + IP + behavior as a composite
- AI-powered risk scoring is standard on major platforms
- Detection now examines Canvas noise patterns and WebGL rendering micro-differences
- JS execution timing differences are being analyzed
[Verified: todetect.net + expressvpn.com, 2026]

## Legal & TOS Risks

### TOS Violation Consequences
- **Account termination**: Permanent bans with no appeal
- **Loss of data**: All content, followers, connections, earnings lost
- **Mass bans**: Linked accounts can trigger simultaneous bans across all accounts
- **Revenue loss**: >50% of marketplace/social suspensions are from technical/behavioral signals, not content
[Verified: antidetectbrowser.org + ceotodaymagazine.com, 2026]

### Legal Risk Assessment
- **TOS violation is not criminal**: Operating multiple accounts violates terms of service, not law
- **Civil liability**: Platforms can pursue civil damages in extreme cases (fraud, impersonation)
- **EU AI Act (August 2026)**: Companies required to label AI-generated content — may affect content operations
- **FTC disclosure**: Affiliate relationships must be disclosed regardless of account structure
- **CFAA risk**: Using anti-detect tools to circumvent access restrictions could theoretically trigger Computer Fraud and Abuse Act issues, though no significant enforcement against multi-accounters
[Blog: upcounsel.com + multistate.us, 2026]

### Risk Mitigation
1. Never use multiple accounts for fraud, impersonation, or manipulation
2. Always disclose affiliate relationships per FTC guidelines
3. Keep separate accounts for separate genuine purposes (not fake consensus)
4. Don't create accounts solely to evade bans
5. Maintain genuine, valuable content on all accounts
6. Accept that account loss is an operational risk — never put all revenue on one platform

## Practical Recommendations

### Phase 1: Single Account (Month 1-3)
- One Reddit account, one Medium account, one LinkedIn profile
- Build genuine reputation on each
- No anti-detect tools needed
- Cost: $0

### Phase 2: Light Scaling (Month 4-6)
- GoLogin free tier for browser profile isolation
- 2-3 accounts per platform max
- Residential proxy for IP diversity ($5-15/mo)
- Cost: $5-15/mo

### Phase 3: Full Scaling (Month 7+, after $500+/mo revenue)
- AdsPower or Multilogin for advanced fingerprinting
- Dedicated proxies per account
- Account warming protocols
- Cost: $50-200/mo
- Only justified when revenue supports the investment
