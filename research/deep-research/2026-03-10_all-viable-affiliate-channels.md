# All Viable Channels for Automated Affiliate Marketing

**Research Date**: 2026-03-10
**Purpose**: Exhaustive inventory of every platform/channel suitable for automated affiliate marketing, ranked by combined score of ease of automation, conversion potential, and account durability.

---

## Scoring Methodology

Each channel is rated 1-10 on three axes:
- **Ease of Automation (EA)**: API availability, scheduling tools, browser automation feasibility, content format simplicity
- **Conversion Potential (CP)**: How well the audience converts on affiliate links, buyer intent, trust level
- **Account Durability (AD)**: Anti-spam enforcement, account ban risk, ability to run multiple accounts, platform tolerance

**Combined Score** = EA x CP x AD (max 1000)
